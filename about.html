Who We Are

Garissa FarmCare Agrovet was founded in 2012 by agricultural expert Mohamed Abdi to address the shortage of quality farm inputs in Northeastern Kenya.

What began as a small shop has grown into Garissa County's most trusted supplier of veterinary medicines, crop protection products, and animal feeds.
